Education Levels

Our Learning Roadmap

A balanced curriculum focused on project-based learning, inquiry, and critical thinking across all age groups.

Nurturing Wonder and Core Competencies

During the primary years, we emphasize basic literacy, analytical skills, and emotional growth. Children explore subjects through collaborative projects and play-centered scientific activities.

  • Core Focus: English Language Arts, Foundational Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Creative Writing.
  • Special Programs: Introductory Coding (Block-based), Speech and Drama, Ceramic Art.
  • Development: Social adjustment, motor skill refining, team-based tasks.

Deepening Inquiry and Analytical Thinking

Middle schoolers transitioning to adolescence are guided to ask deep questions, design science experiments, and construct argumentative essays. We foster self-guided research habits.

  • Core Focus: Pre-Algebra, Physical and Life Sciences, World History, Modern Foreign Languages (French/Spanish).
  • STEAM Focus: Robotic assemblies, basic web layout programming, and scientific lab rotations.
  • Activities: Youth Parliament debates, spelling bees, inter-house academic quiz leagues.

Preparing for College and Global Leadership

Our high school program offers honors and advanced placement options, preparing graduates to succeed in selective universities worldwide. College counselors provide comprehensive pathway advising.

  • Core Pathways: STEM (Calculus, Physics, Chemistry, CS) or Humanities (Literature, Economics, History, Psychology).
  • Honors Options: College Board AP Courses, Senior Thesis projects, and Lab Assistantships.
  • Milestones: SAT/ACT test prep, university mock interviews, career job shadowing internships.
